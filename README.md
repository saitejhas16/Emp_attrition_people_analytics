# 📊 Employee Attrition & People Analytics Dashboard

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing employee attrition using a **data-driven People Analytics approach**.  
The goal is to move beyond basic reporting and uncover:

- **Where attrition is happening**
- **Why employees are leaving**
- **Which employees are at risk**
- **What actions can be taken**

The project combines:
- 📘 Python (Jupyter Notebook) for analysis  
- 📊 Power BI for interactive dashboarding  

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Employee attrition is a critical challenge for organizations.  
Traditional HR reporting often answers *what happened*, but fails to explain:

> ❓ Why are employees leaving?  
> ❓ Which factors actually influence attrition?  
> ❓ Where should the company act proactively?

This project aims to answer these questions using structured analytics.

---

## 🧠 Approach & Methodology

The project follows a structured **end-to-end analytics workflow**:

### 1️⃣ Data Understanding & Cleaning
- Loaded HR dataset
- Handled structure, data types, and consistency
- Converted encoded categorical variables into meaningful labels
- Created readable features for analysis

---

### 2️⃣ Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Analyzed workforce distribution
- Identified attrition concentration across:
  - Departments  
  - Job Roles  
  - Tenure  

👉 Focus: *Where attrition exists*

---

### 3️⃣ Behavioral & Satisfaction Analysis
- Analyzed key drivers:
  - Job Satisfaction  
  - Work-Life Balance  
  - Environment Satisfaction  
  - Relationship Satisfaction  
- Created a **Satisfaction Index** (combined metric)

👉 Focus: *Why attrition happens*

---

### 4️⃣ Correlation & Risk Segmentation
- Performed correlation analysis to identify relationships
- Built **risk segmentation model**:
  - High Risk  
  - Medium Risk  
  - Low Risk  

👉 Focus: *Who is likely to leave*

---

### 5️⃣ Data Preparation for Visualization
- Exported cleaned dataset for Power BI
- Included engineered features:
  - `SatisfactionIndex`
  - `RiskLevel`
  - `Attrition_Flag`

---

## 📊 Dashboard Overview (Power BI)

The dashboard is designed to answer **four key business questions**:

### 1️⃣ What is happening?
- Attrition Rate  
- Total Employees  
- Employees Left  
- Avg Tenure (Attrition cases)  

---

### 2️⃣ Where is attrition happening?
- Attrition by Department  
- Attrition by Job Role  
- Attrition by Tenure  

---

### 3️⃣ Why is attrition happening?
- Behavioral factors:
  - Job Satisfaction  
  - Work-Life Balance  
  - Environment  
- **Key Influencers Visual** (Power BI ML-based insights)

---

### 4️⃣ Where should we act?
- Risk Distribution  
- High-risk employee identification

## 🔍 Key Insights

- 📉 Attrition is highest among **early-tenure employees**
- 🧠 Behavioral factors (satisfaction, work-life balance) are **stronger drivers than salary**
- ⏳ Employee tenure has a strong negative relationship with attrition
- ⚠️ High-risk employees can be proactively identified using segmentation
- 📊 Combined metrics (like Satisfaction Index) provide better insights than individual variables
